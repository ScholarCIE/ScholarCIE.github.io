Zuchen Li

Team Lead @ SOCIE

Hi! I’m Zuchen Li, the founder of Scholarcie (SOCIE) and a passionate undergraduate at the University of Michigan. My studies span History, Statistics, Meteorology, and Computer Science, where I revel in uncovering the unique intersections of these fields. My prior research includes conducting CFD simulations on airborne particles at the Environment-Enhancing Lab and leading a team on a machine learning project based on meteorological data to predict flight delays. Presently, I am a summer research intern employing deep learning methods to explore the complex relationships between aerosols and clouds at GripS.

The inspiration to create SOCIE stemmed from challenges I faced during my undergraduate research: many students are passionate yet unsure about how to begin their research journey. Despite interactions with professors or mentors, there is often a lack of clear guidance from experienced peers. Consequently, I assembled our SOCIE LEAD team to establish this initiative, aimed at assisting young, enthusiastic explorers in navigating the world of research more effectively. Contact me at: zuchenli@umich.edu
